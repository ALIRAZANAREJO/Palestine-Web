Today Broken Cities Of Palestine

Jerusalem

The Arab League is set to hold an emergency meeting to discuss an Israeli police raid on the Al-Aqsa Mosque in Jerusalem that left at least 12 Palestinians injured, as Israeli Prime Miniser Benjamin Netanyahu said he was working to “maintain the status quo” at the holy site.

Bethlehem

The raid on Monday morning followed several others across the territory, which saw dozens of arrests and the shooting in the neck of a 17-year-old boy. Among towns hit was Bethlehem.

Jericho

The Israeli military initially said in a statement that it had killed seven men in total, including two responsible for an attempted shooting attack in Jericho on January 28, and five others who had engaged the army in fighting during the raid.

Beit Jala

In March 2023, a significant incident occurred near Beit Jala, where a 15-year-old Palestinian boy attacked Israeli security personnel at a checkpoint. The attack took place at the tunnel checkpoint south of Jerusalem, close to Beit Jala in the occupied West Bank

Beit Sahour

In October 2023, the town of Beit Sahour experienced heightened tensions amidst the broader conflict in the region. The Citadel, a local restaurant and community center, became a focal point for the community as the war in Gaza seemed to be drawing closer

Hebron

In August 2023, a shooting attack near Hebron resulted in the death of an Israeli woman and serious injury to a man, with the Israeli Prime Minister attributing the attack to Iranian influence

Ramallah and al-Bireh

In December 2023, Israeli forces conducted intense raids in Ramallah and al-Bireh, part of a larger operation in the West Bank, seizing $2.5 million and resulting in clashes and casualties

Nablus

In February 2023, a military incursion by Israel into Nablus resulted in a firefight and the deaths of 11 Palestinians, with separate investigations highlighting the targeting of civilians

Tulkarem

In September 2023, Israeli forces conducted a large-scale raid on Tulkarem’s Nur Shams refugee camp, resulting in the death of a young Palestinian man and extensive damage to the camp’s infrastructure

Jenin

In July 2023, a major Israeli military operation in Jenin, dubbed “Operation Home and Garden,” resulted in the deaths of at least 12 Palestinians and injuries to 100 others

Rafah

In October 2023, Rafah was subjected to an Israeli offensive, resulting in nearly 1,200 Palestinian casualties and warnings from Jordan and Germany about worsening humanitarian conditions

Khan Yunis

In December 2023, the Israeli Defense Forces launched a significant air and ground operation in Khan Yunis, resulting in extensive casualties and damage
