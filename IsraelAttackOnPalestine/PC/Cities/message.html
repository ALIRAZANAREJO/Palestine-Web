Message from Hazrat Muhammand (SAWA)

According to Islamic tradition, there is an event known as the "Night Journey and Ascension" (Isra and Miraj) in which the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) was said to have been taken on a miraculous journey by Allah. During this journey, it is believed that the Prophet visited various sacred places, including Masjid al-Aqsa in Jerusalem. The event of Isra and Miraj holds great spiritual significance for Muslims, and it is mentioned in various Hadith (sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad) and historical accounts. Here's a brief overview of the event

1: Isra

Isra refers to the miraculous night journey of the
Prophet Muhammad from the Masjid al-Haram in
Mecca to Masjid al-Aqsa in Jerusalem.
It is said that the journey was facilitated by the angel Gabriel.

2: Miraj

Miraj, which means "ascension," refers to the spiritual
ascent of the Prophet Muhammad through the heavens,
culminating in his direct encounter with Allah.

3: Visit to Masjid al-Aqsa

It is believed that upon arriving at Masjid al-Aqsa,
the Prophet Muhammad led a congregation of prophets in prayer.
This event symbolizes the unity of prophethood
and the continuity of divine guidance.

4: Ascension to the Heavens

After his visit to Masjid al-Aqsa, the Prophet Muhammad
ascended through the heavens, meeting various prophets
and receiving instructions from Allah.

5: of Divine Messages

During the Miraj, the Prophet received important instructions
and revelations from Allah, including the command for
Muslims to perform the five daily prayers.

the Isra and Miraj is profound for Muslims.

religious significance for Muslims, interpretations
